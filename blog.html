Lauren's Ski Adventures 2024

Crystal Mountain, Washington

This year, I had an amazing experience skiing at Crystal Mountain in Washington. The slopes were challenging, and the views of Mount Rainier were absolutely breathtaking.

Copper Mountain, Colorado

Copper Mountain in Colorado offered some of the best skiing conditions I have ever experienced. The terrain is varied, catering to all levels of skiers, and the atmosphere was vibrant and welcoming.

Whistler, British Columbia

Whistler in British Columbia is a must-visit for any skiing enthusiast. The expansive resort, combined with the quaint village, makes it a perfect destination for both skiing and relaxation.

Mt. Bachelor, Oregon

Mt. Bachelor in Oregon was an adventure of a lifetime. The powder was fresh, and the trails were exhilarating. It's definitely a place I will return to in the future.
